What motivated me to take this class?

The older I've gotten, the more uncertain I've become about my future. I went to college for 6 years doing a digital arts program called Simulation, Animation
and Gaming. I always liked the idea of being a part of a game development studio as a kid because I love video games. Over time, my passion for
3D modeling, texturing, rigging, animating, etc. had completely evaporated and I don't really know why. That's when I figured it was time to throw in the towel and
go find a job doing something else. I was 24 years old and hadn't even worked a job yet. When I heard Amazon was opening a new fulfillment center really close to
where I live, I figured it would've at least been a good first step and a way to finally start making some money.

To my surprise, a little over a year later, Amazon offers a program with Kenzie Academy for web development. I figured I might as well take the opportunity since
I have a little bit of experience with HTML and CSS thanks to the one class in high school and the one class in college I took for it. I'm hoping this course can sort
of serve as a new beginning for me since I've been having a bit of an existential crisis for the past few years. The prospect of getting a job in tech does sound nice
so I suppose that's what motivated me to take this course the most.
